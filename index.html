Introduction

History is a crucial subject that provides us with a comprehensive understanding of the past.
History is a subject that many students struggle to find engaging or interesting.
Game-based learning is an educational approach that uses games to teach a particular subject or skill.
Digital Learning resources utilized in the classroom for learning

Project Context

The Researchers intends to develop a digital game-based learning tool for fifth-grade
students to improve their comprehension and help students learn about Philippine history
in an engaging and interactive way. the project aims to promote motivated and active learning among students.
Through the game, students can explore historical characters and concepts in a more immersive and enjoyable way,
while also developing a stronger sense of cultural identity.

Project Purpose

The purpose of this study is to provide a game based learning tool that motivates grade 5 students
in learning about Philippine history. The game designed to be fun and interactive, and able to keep
the attention of the students while teaching them about the significant events and personalities in
the Philippine history.
